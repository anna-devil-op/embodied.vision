Vision Circle NEW

Eyesight work is literally about changing the way you perceive everything. This is often a challenging process and it can be useful, fun, supportive and heart-warming to share some of the experiences, ups and downs with other people who are experiencing similar things and know what the heck you're talking about.

Where the Somatic Seeing class is all about listening and experiencing, the Vision Circle is all about sharing and communicating your experiences in a held, supportive environment. I look forward to welcoming you and hearing your stories.

Anna’s workshop was a revolution. What she gave me was a paradigm shift in how I think of vision. I was really amazed in the difference in my vision after the workshop. I see space and colour differently. It's a shift in consciousness for me to see seeing by itself for itself as a source of continual nourishment rather than just a pragmatic faculty.
