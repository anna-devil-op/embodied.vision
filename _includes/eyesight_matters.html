Eyesight Matters in Health

This course is for you if you work with people in a therapeutic or creative way. It is essential learning for yoga teachers, psychotherapists, massage therapists, Feldenkrais practitioners, art therapists, play therapists, somatics practitioners... the list goes on.

The Learning

  • Explore how someone's eyesight impacts body, mind and emotion.
  • Learn about common eyesight challenges from a holistic perspective.
  • Understand simple eyesight prescriptions.
  • What does 20/20 vision really mean? How eyesight is measured.
  • How to integrate eyesight into taking a case study.
  • Why it matters that you know if your client or students wear contact lenses.

This is a 6 hour workshop in 3 parts because 6 hours straight online is too much. You need to come to all 3 sessions to cover the material.

PDF course handout included.
