Somatic Seeing

Please join me in my explorations teaching embodied vision education online. These classes are relaxing and perception enhancing, helping you to move into your vision. My aim is that they will feel meditative. The classes will involve some stillness and some gentle movement. Each week we explore a different visual theme, for example, colour, context, texture, depth, details...

The class is drop in, come along when you can. Contact me and I'll send you the class link.

Anna Bambridge offers us in these sessions, without any adornment, many valuable treasures: silence, pause, time, space to feel, to discover for ourselves the consciousness, the attitude, the presence so necessary in our lives and to all our well-being, not just that of our eyes.

Ana Diaz
