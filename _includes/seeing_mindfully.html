Seeing Mindfully

A 2 day live in-person workshop

Would you like to learn gentle relaxation and movement practices which will enhance your eyesight and bring you a greater sense of inner and outer connection?

Seeing is an intricate and sensitive learned process and many things influence its function. The Bates Method of Vision Education is an invitation to gently explore the capacities of your own eyesight. In this workshop, led by Anna Bambridge, we will explore the relationship between our experience of sensory input, primarily from our eyes and our inner somatic experiences of body awareness, thoughts and feelings. It will be a gentle workshop with a light-hearted approach to deep awareness.

“When the mind is at rest, nothing can tire the eyes, when the mind is under strain, nothing can rest them.”

Dr. William H. Bates 1920

If you wear contact lenses please bring what you need to take them out for the workshop.
