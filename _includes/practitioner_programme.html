Bates Vision Education Practitioner Programme

Eyesight

Eyesight is a learned process which depends on internal reflexes and external stimuli. It depends on a weaving of inner and outer experiences.

Eyesight needs the electromagnetic energy of light from the sun and physical, mental, emotional, social and spiritual awareness. A dynamic relating of all these elements permeates healthy vision.

In the Embodied Vision Bates Method Practitioner Programme we explore and engage with all these aspects of vision through supported experiential study.

5 day in-person modules

Each module will weave in the essential elements of light, rest, movement, memory and imagination. Through practical sessions students will work with each other and as a group to learn how to teach Bates Method Vision Education.

The modules also provide

  • a supportive space to explore your own eyesight
  • professional awareness of vision and Vision Education practices
  • clinical experience
  • in-person experience for teachers who have learned online

The modules can be taken separately but also layer together, in any order, to form a complete professional training with over 200 hours of contact time. Students who successfully complete all six modules and the homework requirements will become certified Bates Vision Education teachers.

Programme Director - Anna Bambridge

Anna first encountered the potential of natural eyesight work in 1996 and has been immersed in her own vision journey ever since.

She graduated from the College of Vision Education as a professional Bates Vision teacher in 2006. Her teachers there were Aileen Whiteford and Margaret Montgomery, part of a teaching lineage that is unbroken from W.H. Bates.

Anna has continually developed her vision work and has deepened her understanding through workshops and individual lessons in-person with Anthony Attenborough, Brigitte Cavadias, Peter Grunwald, Nina Hutchings, Roberto Kaplan, Fernanda Leite Ribeiro, Peter Mansfield, Mikolaj Markiewicz, Uschi Ostermeier, Meir Schneider and Esther van der Werf.

She has over 500 hours in-person one to one teaching experience as well as having taught many group workshops including at the International Holistic Vision Conference.

Online she has presented workshops for Redefine Vision and the Association of Vision Educators. She has been on panels with Gloria Ginn, Jacob Liberman, Daniela Matuchova, Claudia Muehlenweg and Nathan Oxenfeld. She has been a guest on the Better Eyesight and Naked Eye podcasts.
